## The challenge
- **Theme:** AI Agents — intelligent workflows, automation, real AI applications.
- Build with **AMD Developer Cloud, ROCm, cloud-accessible AMD GPUs**, exploring next-gen AI applications, infrastructure, and performance experimentation.
- Full challenge details "coming soon" at capture — expect deeper experimentation, more hands-on GPU access, performance-focused AI development.

## Technology & access
- **AMD AI Developer Cloud** — on-demand **AMD Instinct MI300X** GPUs (train, fine-tune, benchmark, deploy). **$100 in cloud credits** for AMD AI Developer Program members.
- **ROCm** — AMD's open-source GPU computing platform (PyTorch/TensorFlow on AMD, CUDA→AMD porting, HPC AI/ML).
- **AMD AI Academy** — courses, tutorials, docs, labs.
- **DeepLearning.AI Pro** — 1-month complimentary membership.
- **AMD Expert Access** — engineers via community channels + office hours.
- Fully cloud-based — no local hardware setup required.

> ⚠️ To unlock credits and be approved: **sign up for the AMD AI Developer Program** ("Sign up with AMD").

## Who can join
Everyone with a passion for AI is welcome, any experience level. Solo or team (find teammates on Discord — where most of the hackathon happens).

## What to submit
**Basic info:** Project Title · Short Description · Long Description · Technology & Category Tags
**Cover & presentation:** Cover Image · Video Presentation · Slide Presentation
**App hosting & code:** Public **GitHub repository** · Demo Application Platform · Application URL
- Submissions must be **original, open source, MIT-licensed** (lablab.ai standard).
- Deadline always current on the Event Schedule tab (local timezone).

## Prizes
- 🥇 **1st Place: $5,000** — top overall project
- 🥈 **2nd Place: $3,000** — runner-up
- 🥉 **3rd Place: $2,000** — top finalist
- Project recognition: possible showcase on AMD social channels / at AMD developer events.

## Judging criteria
1. **Application of Technology** — how effectively the chosen model(s) are integrated.
2. **Presentation** — clarity and effectiveness.
3. **Business Value** — impact and practical value; business fit.
4. **Originality** — uniqueness, creativity, demonstrated behaviors.
